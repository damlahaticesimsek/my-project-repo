Pita Chips Salsa

Body Building | 20min | 97cal

    • Rather than opting for high-fat nacho chips, bake your own pita chips and add salsa. Salsa is a low-calorie condiment that makes sticking to your diet a breeze.

    Ingredients

    2 large whole wheat pita's

    2 tbsp. olive oil

    Sea salt to taste

    1 tsp. garlic salt

    1 tsp. garlic powder

    Directions

    Step 1

    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F

    Step 2

    Brush one side of each pita pocket with olive oil and then sprinkle with sea salt, garlic salt, and garlic powder.

    Step 3

    Slice the pitas into bite-sized pieces and then place in the oven on a non-stick tray for 15-20 minutes, or until lightly brown.

    Step 4

    Remove from the oven to cool for 15-20 minutes and then serve with your favorite salsa.

Chocolate Puff Christmas Tree

Body Building | 45min | 450cal

    • A chocolate and hazelnut pull-apart festive Christmas tree! A delightful and delicious treat to serve around the holiday season.

    Ingredients

    2 x 320g Jus-Rol™ Chilled Ready Rolled Puff Pastry Sheets

    4 tablespoons chocolate-hazelnut spread

    1 egg, beaten

    Icing sugar, for dusting

    Directions

    Step 1

    Preheat the oven to 200°C (180°C for fan assisted ovens), Gas Mark 6. Line a baking sheet with baking paper.

    Step 2

    Unroll one of the puff pastry sheets and place on the baking sheet. Spread the chocolate-hazelnut spread over the puff pastry sheet. Unroll the second puff pastry sheet and place on top of the first one.

    Step 3

    Use a star-shaped cookie cutter to cut out a star from one corner of the layered pastry and set aside. Cut the remaining layered pastry into the shape of a Christmas tree (triangle) with a wide base and trunk. Pull away the excess pastry.

    Step 4

    Lightly score 2 lines down the middle of the triangle to form a tree trunk approximately 3cm wide. Using the trunk as a guide, cut branches into the sides of the triangle, approximately 2cm wide. Leave the top 5cm as this will be covered with the pastry star.

    Step 5

    Starting at the top, twist each branch away from you, giving two turns to each branch.

    Step 6

    Place the pastry star on top of the tree, securing with a little beaten egg. Brush the star and tree with beaten egg.

    Step 7

    Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until risen and golden brown. Cool for a few minutes before carefully transferring to a wire rack to cool. Transfer to a serving plate or board and dust with icing sugar to serve.
